Butter Chicken

Description :

Butter Chicken (also known as Murgh Makhani) is a rich, creamy, and flavorful North Indian dish made with marinated chicken cooked in a mildly spiced tomato-based sauce.

It’s one of the most popular Indian dishes worldwide, loved for its smooth texture and balance of spices.

Ingredients :

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

Steps :

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for 30 mins (or more).
  2. Cook Chicken: Grill or pan-fry marinated chicken until cooked. Set aside.
  3. Make Sauce: In a pan, melt butter. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  4. Add Spices & Tomato: Add cumin, garam masala, and tomato puree. Cook for 10 mins.
  5. Blend (optional): For a smoother sauce, blend the mixture and return to pan.
  6. Add Cream & Chicken: Stir in cream, add chicken, simmer for 10 mins.
  7. Garnish: Sprinkle coriander and a drizzle of cream. Serve hot.
